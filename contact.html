Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect in the first session?

The first session is an opportunity for us to get to know each other. We'll discuss what brings you to therapy, your history, and your goals. I'll explain my approach and we'll determine together if we're a good fit for working together.

How long are sessions?

Standard sessions are 50 minutes. For ISTDP work, I sometimes offer extended sessions of 90 minutes or longer, depending on clinical need and mutual agreement.

Do you offer telehealth sessions?

Yes, I offer secure video sessions for clients who prefer or require remote therapy. Telehealth can be just as effective as in-person therapy for many concerns.

What are your fees and do you accept insurance?

Please contact me directly for current fee information. I can provide superbills for out-of-network reimbursement. A limited number of reduced-fee slots are available for those with financial need.

Is everything I share confidential?

Yes, with rare legal exceptions (such as imminent danger to yourself or others), everything discussed in therapy is strictly confidential. Your privacy and trust are paramount to the therapeutic relationship.
