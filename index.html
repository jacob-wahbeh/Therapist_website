Lasting Change That Gets to the Heart of the Matter

I'm Jacob Sadan, an associate marriage and family therapist and life coach dedicated to helping individuals navigate life's challenges with courage and clarity. My practice is built on the belief that true healing happens when we address not just symptoms, but the whole person: mind, heart, and spirit.

Whether you're struggling with anxiety, depression, relationship issues, or searching for deeper meaning, I'm here to walk alongside you on your journey toward wholeness.

My Therapeutic Approach

Combining evidence-based techniques with faith-informed wisdom for lasting transformation

ISTDP

Intensive Short-Term Dynamic Psychotherapy is a powerful, research-backed approach that helps uncover and process buried emotions, leading to rapid and lasting change.

Faith-Integrated Therapy

For those who desire it, therapy that honors and incorporates Orthodox Christian spirituality, connecting psychological healing with your faith journey.

Compassionate Care

A warm, non-judgmental therapeutic relationship where you feel truly seen, heard, and supported as you work through life's difficulties.
